Solar Energy for Schools in Africa

Simple Solution for a Bright Future ☀️

What is Solar Energy?

Solar energy is power we get from the sun. Solar panels change sunlight into electricity.

Why Schools Need Solar Energy

Many schools in Africa have electricity problems. Solar energy helps schools get light, power computers, and study without interruption.

Advantages

• Free energy from the sun
• Clean and environmentally friendly
• Reduces electricity costs
• Helps students learn better

Conclusion

Solar energy is a simple and smart solution. It can improve education and support development in Africa.
